This resource aims to support practitions deploying differential privacy in practice. It endeavours to leave the reader with practical intuition, responsible guidance and practical case studies of privacy budgets used in practice by organisations today.

This registry lists multiple differential privacy (DP) deployments, with a focus on their privacy budget. It provides examples of DP usage with specified epsilon, delta, and rho values, illustrating their significance and application in real-world scenarios.
